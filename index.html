Museum in your home.

Atelier is a members’ club for those passionate about art. Members get access to a rotating collection of art and, in the comfort of their own homes, enjoy a new work of art every month.

APPLY NOW
DISCOVER

You get a new art experience every month!

You get high-quality art to hang on your walls and discover talented artists living and working today. Different styles, different formats, different media. You will never receive the same work twice.

LEARN

You learn about art, history, new trends, various movements and styles.

You train your eye and better understand what kind of art you would like to have in your own collection.

SUPPORT

By being a member of Atelier you become a patron of emerging artists.

Since the majority of works in the Club Collection are purchased directly from the artists or from galleries representing them.

How it works

It’s very simple.
You pay a monthly membership fee and receive a new work of art every month. When a new work is delivered, the previous one is collected.

For more details, please read our Club Rules. This document covers all aspects of the Club, including eligibility criteria, insurance & security, art collection, membership, fees etc.

Additional Benefits

Atelier is proud to offer its members additional benefits. These include:

- An exclusive community channel for Club Members - to engage, discuss, share your thoughts, opinions, and suggestions for new acquisitions.

- Exclusive discount on all Food & Drinks in the Atelier @Osprey - a unique art exhibition space and cafe in Central London (launch date to be announced soon).

- Discount on art acquisitions made through Atelier Club.

- Exclusive Art Dinners with fun and enjoyable talks on art history, art business, art law and other topics by well-known ‘mystery guests’: artists, curators, collectors, dealers, critics and quite often celebrity speakers.
