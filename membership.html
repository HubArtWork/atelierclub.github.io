Membership

Here’s how you can become a member and why you’ll want to.

Joining Process
  • 1. Click “Apply Now” and fill in the online application form. Please, make sure you satisfy our eligibility criteria before applying.
  • 2. Once your application has been reviewed and approved, you will receive an email confirmation with a link to set up your direct debit.
  • 3. Once you have paid the joining fee and set up your direct debit, your membership will be activated and we will contact you to arrange your first art delivery.
